How To Use

  1. Add ngRadio plugin after your AngularJS.

    <script src="angular.min.js"></script>
    <script src='ngRadio.min.js'></script>

  2. Add ngRadio module dependency to your app.

    angular.module('yourApp', ['ngRadio']);

  3. Use the avaiable directive to show the plugin directly on your view

    <ng-radio ngr-source="yourList"></ng-radio>

  4. Adjust your radio options.

    <ng-radio ngr-source="yourList" ngr-enableNavigation="true" ngr-addStopButton="true"></ng-radio>
